About Us

Our History

SRJ Photography Studio started in 2018 as a small photography business in Mogadishu. Over the years we have grown into a full studio that takes photos for weddings, portraits, events, fashion, nature and drones.

Mission and Vision

Mission: To capture important moments in people's lives through quality photography.

Vision: To be a trusted photography studio known for great service and good pictures.

Why Choose Us: We have years of experience, a friendly team, and affordable packages for every client.
